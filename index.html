Service & Pricing

You can count on me to make your car looking brand new! I offer three different services for all sizes!

Interior Detail - Stage One

Pricing

Coupes / Sedans: $50/$60
Small Trucks & SUVs: $80
3rd Row SUVs, Full Size Trucks, & Minivans: $100

In the Stage One package, the basics are done to the vehiclee, removing the clutter and giving it a new look once again.

In this package will include:

  • Completely removing any loose trash inside the vehicle
  • Complete and deep vacuum of the whole interior
  • Cleaning of the door jambs
  • Clean all windows inside and out, including the windshield
  • Adding a UV protectant to all interior plastics, and giving a slight shine

Interior Detail - Stage Two

Pricing

Coupes / Sedans: $90/$100
Small Trucks & SUVs: $130
3rd Row SUVs, Full Size Trucks, & Minivans: $150
*Note: A $10 fee is applied to the price for excessive pet hair.

With the Stage Two package, it will include all the services listed in the Stage One package, added with these additional services:

  • Stain removal on carpet, seats, and floor mats
  • Steam cleaning of the hard plastics
  • Shampoo of the seats, carpet, and floor mats

Additional Services:

Pricing

Leather Cleaning and Treatment: $30
  • All leather is deep cleaned, and then a conditioner is applied to the seats to keep them looking clean
Engine Detail: $50
  • Deep, thorough, but careful cleaning of the engine bay, and then applying a coating for protection
